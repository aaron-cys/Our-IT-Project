Group Reflection

Summary

One thing that everyone seems to collectively agree on in our reflection is how we have been working hard on improving our consistency in terms of work and the communication that we have amongst each other. We all generally agreed from Assignment 2 how we needed to improve our consistency in work and that point still stands throughout Assignment 3, albeit, much better than it was in comparison to Assignment 2.

Communication as itself was overall the same as it was as Assignment 2, however we were arguably less communicative to each other this time around. Although we lost a team member part way into beginning our Assignment, they were sure to communicate this change to everyone else so we were able to sort out roles and the redistribute some of the work again which allowed us to continue on our work without much confusion or time lost.

One thing that the group seemed to overall agree on in terms of an area for improvement would be our organization amongst the group. Certain parts of the assignment required prior work to be completed which caused a bit of confusion. This was also evident in the audit trail on the Github repository. However, with the right communication at the right times, we were able to address these issues quickly and overcome them.

As a collective, we all agree that were all surprised at how well we were able to complete the assignment in the timeframe given with even a few days to spare on certain tasks. This vast improvement compared to Assignment 2 is definitely noteworthy in everyone’s eyes despite any potential detriments we encountered along the way.

Finally, everyone in the group generally agreed that patience and trust were learnt in this Assignment. Although we did learn this from Assignment 2, Assignment 3 solidified this lesson within everyone. How we all needed to trust and be patient with one another if were to complete this project as a group as efficiently as possible.
